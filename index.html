10.New Leisure Time – Neue Freizeit

The construction of the first state railway from Brunswick to Wolfenbüttel in 1838 can definitely be seen as the beginning of tourism in Wolfenbüttel. What were called “coffee trips” on the train took passengers to the town and the Turkish coffee house by the city moat, erected by order of the Ducal railway administration. People strolled along the levelled bastions of the fortress, which were turned into parks, and played tennis on the former ramparts.
